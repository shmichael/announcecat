Move mixer allows you to practice jazz steps so that you can memorize them, and transition freely between different steps.

Recommended usage instructions

  1. Input a set of jazz moves you want to practice. One per line.
  2. Click one of the buttons to generate a sequence of random moves. Your options are to generate a completely random set, a random set with structure (AAAB or ABAB), or to use the moves you provided in the order you provided them.
  3. Select a song that you like and input its bpm (or use the built-in generated cymbals).
  4. Hit "play" in the middle of a phrase in your song.
  5. The app will read out a move. Perform it on the next phrase. That means wait for the current phrase to end, then transition to the new step. During every phrase you'll be performing a step, and the app will give you a step for the next one. (Similar to callouts at the end of the Shim Sham).
